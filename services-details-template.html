Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA)

Our Specialist Disability Accommodation is designed for individuals with significant functional limitations or extensive support needs. This type of housing, funded through the NDIS, aims to empower individuals to live independently, engage in their communities, and exercise choice and control over their lives. We offer brand-new SDA approved accommodation in West Melbourne, Ivanhoe, and Kalkallo. Designed with state-of-the-art facilities, our homes provide exceptional comfort and seamless accessibility, setting a new benchmark for quality living.

Key inclusion list

Support with daily living tasks
Overnight or active support if required
Personal care and routines
Skill building for independence and confidence
Meal preparation and household skills
Shared living with compatible housemates
Medication support

FAQs

The NDIS is the National Disability Insurance Scheme, designed to reform the way disability services are funded and delivered.

The NDIS provides people with a permanent and significant disability (0 – 65 years) access to supports to help them take part in everyday activities and to achieve their individual goals and aspirations.

Our intake process takes around 1 week, unless there is any other reason for delay.

Cosy home and community care charge their clients as per the NDIS price guide.

We have a detailed discussion with the client or their support coordinator regarding their requirements and preferences. This is the part of our intake process. Based on the understanding, we match a suitable support worker to our clients.

How It Works

Get in touch

Enquire online or book a call to see if SIL is right for you.

Initial connection

We learn about your goals, funding and needs, then plan next steps.

Planning and alignment

We meet to understand your routines and supports, and define what great SIL looks like for you.

Transition planning

We create a clear, agreed plan for your home and support team. Nothing moves forward without your approval.

Move-in and settling in

You move in with your team ready from day one. We check in regularly and adjust early if needed.

Ongoing support and stability

We review your supports over time to keep them safe, consistent and aligned with your goals.
