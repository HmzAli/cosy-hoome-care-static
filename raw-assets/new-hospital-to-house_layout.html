14 Day Rapid Hospital Discharge

At Hogan Care, we understand the challenges faced by individuals who have experienced an accident resulting in disability. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support that empowers our clients to regain independence and improve their quality of life. Our unique 14-Day Rapid Discharge Program is designed to facilitate a seamless transition from hospital to home, ensuring that our clients receive the care and support they need during this critical period.

14-Day Rapid Discharge Program

Our program is a collaborative effort with hospitals to ensure that patients are discharged safely and efficiently within 14 days. We focus on bridging the gap between hospital care and home, providing a structured pathway that supports recovery and rehabilitation. Our team works diligently to coordinate with healthcare professionals, ensuring that all medical needs are addressed before discharge.

Personalised Care Planning

At the heart of our program is the development of a personalised care plan tailored to each individual's needs. Our Registered Nurse (RN) Team collaborates with hospital staff to create a comprehensive plan that outlines the necessary medical, therapeutic, and support services required post-discharge. This plan serves as a roadmap for recovery, detailing everything from medication management to physical therapy and beyond.

Discharge & Transition Day

On the day of discharge, our team is on hand to ensure a smooth transition. We coordinate transportation, assist with settling into the home environment, and provide any necessary equipment or modifications to accommodate the client's needs. Our goal is to make this transition as stress-free as possible, allowing clients to focus on their recovery.

Post-Transition Support

Our commitment to our clients extends beyond the initial discharge. We offer ongoing support to ensure that the transition to home life is successful. This includes regular check-ins, access to a network of healthcare professionals, and assistance with any challenges that may arise. We also provide support in finding suitable accommodation if needed, ensuring that our clients have a safe and comfortable living environment.

What is involved

Our Core Functions

  • Our Customer Relations Team collaborates with hospital social workers, support coordinators, and discharge planners to explore and secure the best care and accommodation options for patients.
  • We initiate planning before the patient’s care plan is formally assessed and approved to ensure no delays in their transition.

How we work alongside hospitals

  • Reduces delays in discharge by addressing care and accommodation needs early
  • Frees up hospital resources by minimising bed-blocking.
  • Allows hospital staff to focus on patient care while we handle logistics.

Our Core Services include:

  • NDIS Supported Independent Living (SIL): Flexible and personalised housing options for individuals with disabilities.
  • We initiate planning before the patient’s care plan is formally assessed and approved to ensure no delays in their transition.
  • TAC-Funded Support: Specialised care for patients recovering from transport-related accidents
  • Therapy Services: Extensive allied health support for rehabilitation and recovery goals.

What's the Process

Week 1

STEP 1

APPROVAL

Our Customer Relations Team collaborates with the patient to explore and secure the best care and accommodation options for patients.

STEP 2

CARE PLAN

Our Registered Nurse (RN)Team collaborates with the hospital care professionals to prepare a detailed personalized post – discharge care plan.

STEP 3

STAFF TRAINING & PREPRATION

Our RN Team prepares training briefs and organizes inductions for support staff to ensure they are equipped to provide high-quality, patient-specific care.

Week 2

STEP 1

ACCOMMODATION SET UP

The Transition and Operations Team prepares the patient’s accommodation, including arranging furniture, equipment and any necessary hires or installations.

STEP 2

ALLIED HEALTH HANDOVER

Our Community Allied Health Team collaborate with hospital allied health professionals to conduct a seamless handover, ensuring all therapy and equipment needs are addressed and signed off.

STEP 3

DISCHARGE & TRANSITION DAY

Our RN and Care Coordinator Teams oversee the discharge process, including arranging transport, managing costs, and ensuring the patient transitions smoothly to their new home or accommodation.

Discharge & Transition Day and Post Transition Support

01

What we do

  • Our RN and Care Coordinator Teams oversee the discharge process, including arranging transport, managing costs, and ensuring the patient transitions smoothly to their new home or accommodation.

02

How it helps hospitals

  • Simplifies the discharge process by handling all logistics.
  • Enables hospital staff to focus on other priorities, knowing the patient’s transition is fully managed.
  • Reduces the stress of last-minute delays or issues on the day of discharge.

03

Post Transition Support: A Commitment to Continued Care

  • We provide regular updates to the hospital for 4 weeks post-discharge, detailing the patient’s condition and transition progress.
  • Our team addresses any concerns or issues that arise during this period, ensuring the patient's transition remains successful.
  • A final handover to the hospital is completed at the end of the 4 weeks.
