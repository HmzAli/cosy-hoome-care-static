Why Choose Us

Registered NDIS Provider

Trusted Care, Delivered with Confidence

As a registered NDIS provider, we meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and compliance. Our participants and families can trust that every service is delivered by qualified professionals who are committed to providing reliable, ethical, and person-centred support.

Supporting Lives Since 2017

Experience You Can Rely On

For years, Cosy Home Care has been helping individuals with disabilities live more independently and achieve their goals. Our proven experience enables us to provide consistent, high-quality support tailored to the evolving needs of participants and their families.

Personalised Support That Puts You First

Your Goals, Your Journey

We understand that every participant is unique. That's why we create individualised support plans that reflect your personal goals, preferences, and aspirations, empowering you to make choices and live life on your own terms.

Experienced Professionals Who Care

Compassion Backed by Expertise

Our dedicated team of Support Workers, Occupational Therapists, and Client Managers work together to deliver exceptional care. We build trusted relationships through compassion, respect, professionalism, and a genuine commitment to achieving positive outcomes.

Quality Accommodation & Independent Living

Safe, Comfortable, and Accessible Homes

We provide modern SDA and SIL accommodation designed to support independence, comfort, and wellbeing. Our homes offer participants the opportunity to live in a supportive environment while maintaining choice, control, and dignity.

Empowering Independence & Community Inclusion

Helping You Thrive Every Day

We support participants to build life skills, increase confidence, and actively engage with their communities. Through meaningful social connections, skill development, and community participation, we help individuals lead fulfilling and empowered lives.
