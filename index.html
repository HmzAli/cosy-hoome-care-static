Why Choose Us

Expertise & Compassionate Care

Our team includes highly trained professionals with experience in Allied Health and hospital systems, ensuring expert care and tailored support. Our dedicated support workers are passionate about enhancing the lives of our participants...

providing personalised care and engaging experiences that make a real difference every single day.

Modern & Fully Equipped SDA & SIL Homes

We offer brand-new, purpose-built SDA and SIL homes, designed with cutting-edge NDIS-approved features to ensure comfort, accessibility, and independence. Our homes provide a safe and supportive environment...

enabling participants to live as independently as possible, with dignity and confidence in their daily lives.

Innovation & Community Impact

We go beyond traditional care, fostering inclusivity and engagement through community-driven initiatives like youth basketball, life skills training, and meaningful social participation. At Cosy Home Care, we aim to build...

an inclusive community and be a leader in innovative disability support that truly changes lives.

Seamless NDIS & Hospital to Home Transitions

We understand the challenges of navigating the NDIS, and we aim to make the transition simple and stress-free for participants and their families. We also specialise in hospital and rehab centre discharges...

ensuring a smooth, efficient, and safe transition into our NDIS-approved disability homes with full support every step of the way.

Empowering Independence

We believe people with disabilities should live as equal citizens, with the opportunity to be as independent as possible. Our services are designed to build capacity, develop life skills...

and support participants in achieving their personal goals, creating a fulfilling and empowered life at home.

Flexible & High-Quality Services

From specialist disability accommodation (SDA) and supported independent living (SIL) to community access, life skills development, and daily living assistance, we provide tailored, high-quality support...

and sustainable services to meet individual needs, ensuring every participant receives the care they truly deserve.
