About Us

Our Story

Established in 2017

The Cosy Home Care journey. We began our mission in 2017, as the NDIS first rolled out across Melbourne. From the start, we focused on inclusive, community based housing for people with disabilities, with particular care for those in the mental health sector.

Our background. We started by delivering direct support services, eventually assisting up to 200 participants with everything from community access to daily living. Over time we saw a real gap in housing. Many people were at risk of homelessness or stuck in hospital because there were not enough suitable, affordable homes. That is why we shifted to focus on Supported Independent Living (SIL), a shared model that lowers costs, protects independence, and keeps people connected to their community.

What makes us different

  • Concierge support model. Instead of rigid schedules, support is flexible and person led. Participants choose when they receive help, whether that is showering, meals, or community outings.
  • Community matching. We recruit high quality staff and carefully match housemates who share similar lifestyles and interests, so each home feels like a genuine community.
  • Strategic collaboration. We do not try to be a one stop shop. We work with developers, allied health, and other providers, and if we are not the right fit we help people find a provider who is.

A registered NDIS provider. We are dedicated to evolving our practices and meeting the highest standards of quality, so we can keep providing safe, innovative, and welcoming housing for the most vulnerable members of our community.

Our Vision

Empowering Independence, Fostering Dignity: Our vision at Cosy Home Care is to be the trusted cornerstone of support for individuals with disabilities, where compassion, respect, and innovation come together to create a warm and welcoming environment that enables every individual to thrive in the comfort and security of their own home.

Our Mission

At Cosy Home Care, our mission is to provide exceptional, person-centred home care services to individuals with disabilities. We are committed to enhancing their quality of life by delivering compassionate and professional care that promotes independence, fosters community inclusion, and upholds the highest standards of dignity and respect. We strive to be the trusted partner in their journey towards a fulfilling, comfortable, and empowered life at home.
