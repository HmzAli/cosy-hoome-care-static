NDIS

Supporting Individuals with Disabilities Through the NDIS

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) supports individuals with disabilities, their families, and carers by providing funding for tailored services and early intervention. Through the NDIS Accommodation Scheme, people with significant disabilities gain access to personalised support, empowering them to achieve their goals and live better lives.

Community Participation: The NDIS often supports activities that promote social inclusion and community involvement. This assistance helps our clients engage more fully in their communities and improve their social inclusion. Cosy Home and Community Care helps individuals navigate the NDIS system, connect with government agencies, and access housing and support services.

Our experienced team assists with:

  • Understanding NDIS eligibility and plans
  • Preparing for NDIS planning meetings
  • Accessing support services covered by NDIS
  • Implementing NDIS plans effectively

At Cosy Home Care, we ensure safety, quality, and personalised care tailored to your unique needs. We build a welcoming community where everybody matters.
