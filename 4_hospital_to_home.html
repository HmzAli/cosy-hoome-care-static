Hospital to Home Discharge

Safe and Seamless Hospital-to-Home Discharges for NDIS Participants

Why Choose Cosy Home Care?

At Cosy Home Care, we specialize in ensuring a smooth discharge from hospital to Supported Independent Living (SIL) or Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA) homes. Our dedicated team provides tailored care plans and expert support to help participants transition safely and confidently, promoting independence, comfort, and peace of mind for both participants and their families.

  • Expert Care Plans: Each participant receives a personalized care plan tailored to their unique needs, ensuring a smooth transition and ongoing well-being.
  • Specialist Support Services: Our team provides 24/7 clinical oversight, advanced medication management, and assistance with daily living, complex care, and rehabilitation.
  • Modern, Accessible Homes: All SIL and SDA homes are thoughtfully designed with modern furnishings and fittings that meet NDIS standards, empowering participants to control their environment for maximum independence and comfort.
  • Community Connection: We promote inclusion through community access programs, life skills development, and activities such as our Limitless League inclusive basketball competition.
  • Collaborative Discharge Planning: We work closely with hospital staff, NDIA planners, support coordinators, and allied health professionals (e.g., occupational therapists, physiotherapists, and behaviour practitioners) to develop comprehensive, tailored discharge plans for each participant.

High-Intensity and Complex Support Services

We deliver specialized care for participants with significant or permanent disabilities, ensuring their needs are met post-discharge. Our highly trained professionals provide:

  • Advanced medication management
  • Continuous monitoring
  • Specialized interventions for challenging behaviours
  • Support for:
    • High Physical Needs
    • Complex Support Needs
    • Reduced Functional Capacity
    • Managing Challenging Behaviours
