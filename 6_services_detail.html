Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA)

Our Specialist Disability Accommodation is designed for individuals with significant functional limitations or extensive support needs. This type of housing, funded through the NDIS, aims to empower individuals to live independently, engage in their communities, and exercise choice and control over their lives. We offer brand-new SDA approved accommodation in West Melbourne, Ivanhoe, and Kalkallo. Designed with state-of-the-art facilities, our homes provide exceptional comfort and seamless accessibility, setting a new benchmark for quality living.
