About Us

Our Story

Established in 2017

The Cosy Home Care Journey: We began our mission in 2017, as the NDIS first rolled out across Melbourne. From the start, we focused on building inclusive, community-based housing, particularly for people in the mental health sector, so no one would be left behind.

Our Company Background: We started by delivering direct support services, assisting up to 200 participants with community access and daily living. We saw a gap: many were at risk of homelessness or stuck in hospital due to a lack of suitable, affordable homes. That’s why we shifted to focus exclusively on Supported Independent Living (SIL)—a shared model that lowers costs, protects independence, and keeps people connected to community.

What Makes Us Different

  • Concierge Support Model: Flexible, person-led support. Participants choose when they receive help, like a concierge for daily life.
  • Community Matching: We carefully match housemates and recruit great staff so each home feels like a real community.
  • Strategic Collaboration: We partner with developers, allied health, and other providers, and we help people find the right fit, even if it’s not us.

Registered NDIS Provider: We’re a registered provider committed to evolving our practices and meeting the highest quality standards to deliver safe, innovative housing for vulnerable community members.
